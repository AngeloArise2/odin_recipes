Chicken Curry

Description

A rich and flavorful chicken curry simmered with aromatic spices, tomatoes, and onions.

Best enjoyed with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in chicken and brown on all sides.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in coconut milk, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
