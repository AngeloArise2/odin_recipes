Greek Salad

Description

A refreshing salad from Greece, packed with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese.

It's perfect as a side dish or a light meal on its own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers (sliced)
  • 3 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 100g feta cheese (cubed)
  • 12 Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Gently toss to mix.
  4. Top with feta cubes and serve fresh.
