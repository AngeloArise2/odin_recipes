Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Soft, chewy cookies loaded with chocolate chips.

A classic treat for any occasion, perfect with a glass of milk or a cup of coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake for 10–12 minutes.
  7. Cool and enjoy!
